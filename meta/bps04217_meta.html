Abstract

Implementation of Good Practice Guide 13 is a strong recommendation for all HMG ICT Systems, and is essentially compulsory for systems that store high impact level data. The goal of a Protective Monitoring system is to ensure that there is a level of operational insight, to ensure that organisations have an understanding of how their IT systems are being used or abused by internal or external agents.
