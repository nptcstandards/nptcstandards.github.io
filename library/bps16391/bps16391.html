Major investigation and public protection

The major investigation and public protection area of APP is currently under development. Modules will be uploaded as they are completed according to the release schedule below, and their publication will result in existing titles being decommissioned. Note: pre-existing titles remain current until further notice.  See the up-to-date list of decommissioned titles.

When complete, the APP on major investigation and public protection will comprise:

Public protection

Core knowledge for protecting the public:

  • Public protection
  • Mental health
  • Managing sexual offenders and violent offenders.

Major investigation

Core knowledge for major investigations:

  • Major incident room standardised administrative procedures
  • Major investigation reviews
  • Major investigation support
  • Managing major investigations.
