Abstract

This document provides UK Policing with a methodology to security, accreditation and risk management which has been designed as an enabler to The UK Policing Vision 2025. In addition to defining an approved methodology to follow, this document also provides appendices of predefined useable baseline product inputs to be leveraged by other policing projects to reduce the time and effort involved in performing their required risk management and assessment work.
