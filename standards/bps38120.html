Abstract

This guide provides the best current practice (BCP) for the preparation, deployment and maintenance of Office Pro Plus 365 applications onto an end user endpoint in a Policing environment. It is intended that by providing guidance that policing organisations can converge on a common methodology and configuration that will facilitate integration into the national strategy for Productivity Services as developed by the National Enabling Programmes (NEP).
